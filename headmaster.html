Headmaster Moraine
Lunar Mage
A potent mage born during a Winter Solstice. Moraine's Lunar Sceptre acts as a catalyst allowing him to weave powerful spells.

Despite his seemingly unlimited potential, Moraine lost his will to fight after the losses incurred while taking down the Dweller of Strife decades ago. As the last Solstice Warrior, he took up the mantle of Zenith Academy's Headmaster in order to train Erlina and Brugaves, who were delivered by the great eagle shortly after the tragedy.
