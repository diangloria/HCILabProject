Teaks
Traveling Historian
Even though the Traveling Historians Guild has been gone for ages, Teaks took it upon herself to bring the tradition back.

Early in her adventure, she acquired her very own Lore Grimoire: a magical history book that writes itself by coming into contact with relics of old. Teaks becomes fast friends with our heroes, who agree to bring any relics they find back to her.

Teaks will often join the party at the campfire and offer to share stories from her growing book.
